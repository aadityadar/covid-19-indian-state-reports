BULLETIN
DATE: 31/01/2020
In the context of Novel corona viral (2019-ncov) epidemic, surveillance and control measures have been strengthened in Kerala. Till date, 1471 persons from Corona affected countries are placed under surveillance, out of which 1421 are under home quarantine and 50 people are admitted in designated isolation facilities. Samples of symptomatic persons have been sent to National institute of virology, Pune. The concerned hospital authorities have reported that all admitted cases are stable. At present, only one positive case has been reported. The second sample result of the student is awaited. A 24x7 call center has been set up in the Directorate of Health services (Contact no. 9072315151, email: controlroomdhskerala@gmail.com)
Since there is an existing alert in the background of present nCorona virus outbreak in the State, the general public is advised to follow the guidelines pertaining to personal and environmental hygiene. The Health department emphasizes the importance of hand washing and use of handkerchief while sneezing/coughing to prevent nCorona virus infection.
